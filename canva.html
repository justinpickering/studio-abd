CANVA PROJECT

ABOUT

The following design aims to transform a two-story workspace into a welcoming and sophisticated environment through the use of ambient lighting, spectacular wall features, and carefully selected finishes. A combination of organic design elements and sustainably sourced materials have been combined to create a simplistic yet elegant design. A contemporary design style flows throughout the office space to maintain a sense of fluidity and balance with the use of a neutral colour palette and green coloured accents, mimicking the colours of the Palm Tree.
