EVERGREEN CANCER HEALING CENTER PROJECT

ABOUT

The following thesis project aims to bridge the gap between healthcare and hospitality through a multifaceted cancer healing center designed for rural patients receiving cancer treatments in Toronto. The Evergreen Cancer Healing Center is a one-of-a-kind facility, placing emphasis on providing accommodations that strive to promote physical and mental well-being in a unique atmosphere.
