WELLNESS FIRST FACILITY PROJECT

ABOUT

The following design aims to create a multifaceted facility that combines both the science of traditional medicine with spiritual and holistic approaches toward mental health. Focus is placed on successfully meshing these two practices into one cohesive design that fosters a comfortable environment, all while creating a brand image of being compassionate, knowledgeable and forward thinking.
