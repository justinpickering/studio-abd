NEW YORK CITY LOFTS PROJECT

ABOUT

The New York City loft project tells the story of ying and yang within the interior space. The interiors are inspired by both the raw industrial elements in the Meat Packing District in New York, and the lush nature that lies just outside the city in Upstate New York. The juxtaposition between the flow and force of nature and the urban environment makes for a dynamic interior. The spaces provides a place of refuge for habitants who want to stay connected to the city bustle, while also enjoying some much needed relaxing time.
