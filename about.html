ABOUT US

ALESSIA BALDASSARRE
PRESIDENT & PRINCIPAL DESIGNER
Alessia earned her Bachelor of Commerce degree from the University of Guelph followed by a Bachelor of Interior Design from Yorkville University. She began her interior design journey in 2015 and throughout the years has honed her skills working closely alongside leaders of the design industry. With the goal of bringing design visions to life, Alessia strives to create spaces that embrace simplicity and elegance through distinctive and personal lifestyle-based interiors. In doing so, she places focus on fostering cohesive spaces that are reflective of company values and client needs. Alessia is currently working towards her NCIDQ certification and is an intern member of ARIDO.
JUSTIN PICKERING
PROJECT MANAGER
Justin graduated from the University of Guelph with a Bachelor of Engineering and specializes in product and software development. Justin plays a key role in the execution and progression of our projects from an IT consulting and trades management perspective. His attention to detail and meticulous approach allows our team to create unique and functional spaces.
MICAELA MONTEMARANO
MARKETING COORDINATOR
Micaela is currently pursuing her Bachelor of Commerce degree from the University of Toronto and is set to graduate in 2025. She has always shared a passion for interior design and through her studies has grown very fond of marketing. As a Studio ABD team member, she has applied her marketing knowledge to support our studio's branding and social media efforts while participating in the design process as an associate designer.

VALUES

VISION
Our vision is to positively contribute to the design industry through innovative and sustainable design practices that promote the health and welfare of people and the planet. With the goal of becoming a globally recognized professional interior design firm, providing creative solutions in the field of residential, commercial and hospitality interiors is the Studio ABD vision.
MISSION
Our mission is to positively influence the lives of all end users of the spaces we design. With a focus on leaving a lasting impression on our clients through our distinct approach, we aim to inspire through our passion for design. Striving to foster the unique and unforgettable feeling that beautifully designed spaces can evoke on people is our primary mission.
VALUE
Our focus is to provide exceptional service that leaves a lasting impression on our clients. In doing so, we place emphasis on originality and creativity by fostering a vision that is unique to our clients wants and needs. With fundamental values of competence, coherence and reliability, we provide a high level of expertise in the interior design field with unparalleled value.
