AZURA BOUTIQUE HOTEL PROJECT

ABOUT

The Azura Luxury boutique hotel tells a story of the flow and force of nature within an interior space. Borrowing from the natural elements surrounding the region of Niagara Falls, Ontario, such as the freshwater lakes and the abundant growth in the escarpment. This multi-dimensional space aims to provide a timeless, elegant and modern home away from home through the use of layered lighting and dimensional textures. The Azura Boutique Hotel is where sustainability meets comfort, and guests are invited to recharge and recenter themselves in the thoughtfully curated interiors.
